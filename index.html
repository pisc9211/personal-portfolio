Who am I?

Hi I'm Paul Chung. I am an aspiring software developer based in NYC. Coming from a non-traditional background (biology degree), I have come to love coding and development enough to change careers. Initally self-taught, I learned the basics of JavaScript/HTML/CSS using the free resources online. I enjoy watching and playing basketball and tennis, can live off of pizza, and love to relax with a hot cup of coffee. I am an optimist, a hard-worker, and a constant learner looking to take on the world one challenge at a time.

I am currently looking for a front-end developer position.
